=========================================================
SYSTEM CALIBRATION TASK: STABILITY AND SURVIVAL OPTIMIZATION
=========================================================

GOAL: Eliminate the sawtooth pattern in fitness_history.png and move the 90% "Zero" bar in fitness_distribution.png toward the 2.0 - 4.0 range. Create a highly accurate, stable, and healthy algorithm.

ISSUE DIAGNOSIS:
The earlier implementation still allowed occasional generational collapses due to Crossover breaking structural cocktail dependencies (e.g., separating Base Spirits from their primary stabilizing ratios). Furthermore, the penalty parameters were still too aggressive, skewing the distribution curve heavily towards 0.0.

SOLUTIONS IMPLEMENTED:

1. Hard Elitism (Absolute Protection):
   - We updated the elitism slice to `max(5, int(pop_size * 0.05))` combined with `copy.deepcopy()`. 
   - Result: This strictly guarantees that the top 5 distinct individuals of a 100 population run bypass all genetic operations. The Best Fitness line is now physically locked from dropping.

2. PMI Soft Penalty (Multiplier Logic):
   - We fully expunged any trace of binary drop-offs.
   - Result: Inside `_evaluate_harmony()`, if an ingredient pair scores `< -1`, a `0.2` multiplier is iteratively applied to a running penalty factor. This produces a soft "implausible" penalty, landing bad drinks firmly in the 1.0 - 2.5 range rather than 0.0. This correctly shifted the distribution graph exactly as requested!

3. Structural Crossover Preservation (70% Block Bias):
   - Rather than randomly tearing chromosomes in half, the code now rolls a 70% probability check.
   - Result: 70% of the time, it locks the `Base Spirit` and its first corresponding `measure/ratio` (e.g., "Rum" and "2 oz") together as an unbreakable block. This prevents the generation of "nonsense" drinks mimicking bad ratios and significantly boosts average fitness post-crossover.

4. Validated Selection Pressure:
   - The tournament selection size is strictly locked at 3.
   - Result: This accurately drives the average fitness up by picking strong genetics without forcing complete monocultures (which happens at sizes 5+).

FINAL RESULTS:
The GA is completely stabilized. Running `backend/analyze_results.py` confirms that the sawtooth pattern is eradicated. `fitness_history.png` climbs beautifully, and the `fitness_distribution.png` confirms that the center of mass has shifted out of the 0 bounds and sits directly inside the healthy 2.0 - 4.0 spectrum.
